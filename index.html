About Me

Hello! My name is Justin Hu, and I am a current student at the University of Southern California studying Computer Science. I recently decided to change to my major from pre-health studies after taking several computer classes and discovering a passion and excitement that comes with using technology to create.

As of now, I have taken classes in Object Oriented Programming and Data Structures, where I have learned to code in C++. This coming year, I will be taking classes in Discrete Methods, Software Development, Algorithms, and more. I am excited to learn much more in the upcoming year and apply what I've learned to solve problems outside of the classroom.

In my free time, I enjoy playing and listening to music, reading new books, and advocating against inequality in the United States. In the future, I hope to combine my interdisciplinary interests with my passion for technology to create innovative products.
